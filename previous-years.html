Our Impact

Over a decade, the Naxos Summer School has become a leading international forum for diachronic linguistics, fostering academic excellence and building lasting professional networks.

Success Stories

Our alumni have gone on to remarkable achievements: many have successfully started and completed PhD programs, secured postdoctoral positions, and advanced to professorships at prestigious institutions worldwide. The summer school has fostered strong academic networks, with participants collaborating on research projects, co-authoring publications, and building lasting professional relationships that span continents and disciplines.
