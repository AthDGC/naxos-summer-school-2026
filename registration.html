Scholarships

A limited number of scholarships will be offered to participants of the 11th Naxos Summer School on Diachronic Linguistics.

At least one scholarship will be awarded prior to the Summer School based on academic merit and the overall quality of the application. In addition, at least one further scholarship will be awarded after the completion of the Summer School, taking into account participants’ academic engagement and contribution during the program.

To apply for a scholarship for the 11th Naxos Summer School on Diachronic Linguistics, please use the following link: https://forms.gle/DdqZ95NXQuTKN7u96

Deadline: Submit your materials by 20 April 2026. You will be notified about the results no later than 25 April 2026.

Best Presentation / Poster Award

The 11th Naxos Summer School on Diachronic Linguistics will present a Best Presentation / Poster Award recognizing outstanding research presented during the program.

The award will be given to the participant whose presentation or poster demonstrates exceptional academic quality, originality, and clarity of presentation. The selection will be made by a committee of instructors and organizers.

The award aims to encourage high-quality research and active participation among early-career researchers.

Student Assistants

A small team of MA students from the Department will assist during the Summer School.

Student assistants will help coordinate daily activities, provide support and assistance to participants and speakers, and contribute to the overall realization of the program.

We are grateful for their valuable contribution to the success of the Summer School.
