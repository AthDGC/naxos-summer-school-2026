Eligibility

The summer school is open to:

  • Advanced Bachelor's students in Linguistics, Languages, Classics, or related fields
  • Master's students focusing on historical linguistics
  • PhD candidates conducting research in diachronic linguistics
  • Early career researchers and postdoctoral fellows

ECTS Credits

Students will be able to earn up to 6 ECTS credits from the summer school upon successful completion of:

  • Active participation in all lectures and workshops
  • Presentation at the workshop/poster session
  • Final project or paper submission

ECTS approval: 8-2-2021 - law: a90.4692/2020
