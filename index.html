Academic Excellence

Bridging Past & Future
in Linguistics

Organized by the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens in collaboration with Arizona State University, University of Osnabrück, and University of Cambridge.

This year's focus, "Comparing Diachronies," explores how ancient and medieval languages evolve through contrastive computational analysis and traditional philology.

AthDGC Research Project

Part of the HFRI-funded project (Grant No. 20577) developing corpus-based valency lexicons. We combine NLP methods with deep linguistic analysis.
