The Program

Comparing Diachronies

The National and Kapodistrian University of Athens, in collaboration with Arizona State University, University of Osnabrück, and University of Cambridge, invites you to a unique academic experience.

Our summer school offers intensive language and linguistic classes focused on Historical & Diachronic Linguistics. We aim to foster a relaxed yet intellectually stimulating atmosphere where students and scholars can discuss the evolution of ancient and medieval languages.

Research Context

Part of the HFRI-funded project: "A Corpus-based Valency Lexicon for a Contrastive and Diachronic Study of Ancient & Medieval Languages" (Grant No. 20577).
