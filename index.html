The Program

About the Summer School

The National and Kapodistrian University of Athens, in collaboration with **Arizona State University**, **University of Osnabrück**, and **University of Cambridge**, coordinates a summer school program, workshop/poster session, and master classes on ancient and medieval languages and linguistics.

The aim is to attract students and scholars to study ancient and medieval languages in a relaxed yet focused and stimulating atmosphere that promotes in-depth analysis and discussion.

AthDGC Research Project

This summer school is part of the Athens Digital Glossa Chronos (AthDGC) project: 'A Corpus-based Valency Lexicon for a Contrastive and Diachronic Study of Ancient & Medieval Languages'.

The project develops corpus-based valency lexicons using innovative computational methods (NLP) integrated with traditional philological expertise.

Funded by the Hellenic Foundation for Research and Innovation (HFRI) (Grant No. 20577).
