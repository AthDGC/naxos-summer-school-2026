Academic Mission

Comparing Diachronies

Coordinated by the **National and Kapodistrian University of Athens**, in partnership with **Arizona State**, **Osnabrück**, and **Cambridge**, this program serves as a premier global hub for diachronic linguistics.

Our focus is the structural evolution of ancient and medieval languages. As part of the **AthDGC Research Project** (HFRI Grant No. 20577), we integrate computational NLP methods with deep philological analysis.
