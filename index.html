The AthDGC
Research Hub

The **11th Naxos Summer School** is an international collaboration between the **National and Kapodistrian University of Athens**, the **University of Cambridge**, **Arizona State**, and **Osnabrück**.

Our focus is the structural evolution of ancient and medieval languages. As a centerpiece of the **AthDGC Research Project** (HFRI Grant No. 20577), we utilize computational **NLP pipelines** and **Stanza-based annotation** to track verb valency patterns across massive diachronic corpora.
