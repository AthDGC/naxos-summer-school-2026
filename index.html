The **11th Naxos Summer School** is coordinated by the **National and Kapodistrian University of Athens**, in partnership with the **University of Cambridge**, **Arizona State**, and **Osnabrück**.

Our core mission is the structural evolution of ancient and medieval languages. As a centerpiece of the **AthDGC Research Project** (HFRI Grant No. 20577), we utilize computational NLP pipelines to track verb valency patterns across diachronic corpora.
