Social Events & Activities

Welcome Reception

Opening evening gathering at a traditional taverna in Naxos Town. Meet fellow participants and faculty in a relaxed setting. Local wine and mezze. Opportunity to experience authentic Greek hospitality and cuisine.

Academic Networking Session

Informal gathering for participants to discuss research interests and potential collaborations. Exchange ideas about diachronic linguistics projects and methodologies. Faculty mentoring opportunities available.

Sunset at Portara

Evening gathering at the Temple of Apollo to watch the legendary Naxos sunset through the ancient marble gateway. One of the most photographed spots in the Cyclades. Short walk from town center.

Day Trip to Nearby Island

Optional excursion to a neighboring Cycladic island (Paros, Delos, or Small Cyclades). Experience island hopping, visit additional archaeological sites, explore different island cultures. Ferry tickets at student rates.

Farewell Dinner

Closing evening celebration featuring traditional Greek music and dancing. Local musicians and dancers showcase Naxian cultural traditions. Certificates presented. Celebration of the summer school experience.

Please Note: Some social activities may require additional costs for transportation, meals, or entrance fees beyond the registration fee. Participants are responsible for these expenses.
