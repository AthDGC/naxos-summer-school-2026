Social Program

We are planning an exciting social program for participants. Please note that the specific schedule is tentative and subject to change.

Confirmed Events

  • Welcome Reception: A chance to meet fellow participants and faculty.
  • Sunset at Portara: A visit to the iconic Temple of Apollo.
  • Day Trip to Nearby Island: Optional excursion to a neighboring Cycladic island (perhaps Paros, Delos, or Small Cyclades).
  • Farewell Dinner: A final gathering to celebrate the school.
