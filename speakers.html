Teaching Approach

Our faculty combines traditional philological expertise with modern computational methods, providing students with a comprehensive understanding of historical linguistics. Classes emphasize:

  • Interactive lectures with Q&A sessions
  • Hands-on workshops with real corpora
  • Small group discussions and analysis
  • Individual feedback on student projects
  • Collaborative research approaches
