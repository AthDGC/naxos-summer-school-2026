Program Schedule

July 19-24, 2026 | Naxos, Greece

Topics Covered

The 11th Naxos Summer School will cover a comprehensive range of topics in diachronic linguistics:

Theoretical Foundations

  • Introduction to diachronic linguistics
  • Comparative methods in language change
  • Corpus linguistics approaches
  • Historical sociolinguistics

Ancient Languages

  • Greek diachrony (Ancient to Modern)
  • Latin and Romance languages
  • Ancient Near Eastern languages
  • Indo-European linguistics

Medieval Languages

  • Medieval Greek and Byzantine texts
  • Old English and Germanic languages
  • Medieval Romance varieties
  • Manuscript studies and philology

Computational Methods

  • Digital humanities approaches
  • Corpus analysis tools
  • AI and machine learning in linguistics
  • Open science and data sharing
